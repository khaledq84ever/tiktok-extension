TikGet

TikTok videos, no watermark, instantly.

Click any TikTok video and TikGet grabs the clean HD or SD file straight to your Downloads folder — no watermark, no signup, no nonsense.

1
Pin TikGet to your toolbar
Click the puzzle icon in the browser toolbar, then click the pin next to TikGet.
2
Open any TikTok video
The Download button slides in over the video. Click and your video lands in Downloads/TikGet/ with no watermark.
3
Or paste a URL into the popup
Click the TikGet icon, paste a TikTok link, choose HD, SD, or MP3 audio only.
